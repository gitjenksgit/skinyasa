About

Skinyasa is a holistic skin and wellness studio founded by Jennifer Smith, a licensed esthetician and Reiki practitioner. We believe true beauty radiates from within — and our treatments are designed to nurture both your skin and your spirit.

Every session is tailored to you. Whether you're here for a targeted skin treatment or a moment of deep relaxation, you'll leave feeling refreshed, balanced, and glowing.

Services

  • Microneedle / Channel

    Stimulates collagen production and enhances product absorption for smoother, firmer skin.

  • Microdermabrasion

    Gently exfoliates the outer layer of skin to reveal a brighter, more even complexion.

  • Hydrofacial

    A multi-step treatment that cleanses, extracts, and hydrates for an instant glow.

  • Dermaplane

    Removes dead skin cells and fine vellus hair for a silky-smooth canvas.

  • Facials

    Customized facials designed around your skin's unique needs and goals.

  • Reiki

    A gentle energy healing practice that promotes deep relaxation and balance.

  • Private Yoga

    One-on-one sessions tailored to your body, breath, and personal wellness journey.
